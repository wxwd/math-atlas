## 题目
设 ${ABCD}$ 为任意给定的四边形,边 ${AB},{BC},{CD},{DA}$ 的中点分别为 $E, F, G, H$ . 证明: 四边形 ${ABCD}$ 的面积 $\leq  {EG} \cdot  {HF} \leq  \dfrac{1}{2}\left( {{AB} + {CD}}\right)  \cdot  \dfrac{1}{2}\left( {{AD} + {BC}}\right)$ .

## 答案
（无）

## 解析
![4_949_254_433_301_0.jpg](images/4_949_254_433_301_0.jpg)

如图, ${S}_{\bigtriangleup {AEH}} = \dfrac{1}{4}{S}_{\bigtriangleup {ABD}},{S}_{\bigtriangleup {CFG}} = \dfrac{1}{4}{S}_{\bigtriangleup {CBD}} \; \Rightarrow  {S}_{\bigtriangleup {AEH}} + {S}_{\bigtriangleup {CFG}} = \dfrac{1}{4}{S}_{ABCD},$

同理可得 ${S}_{\bigtriangleup {BEF}} + {S}_{\bigtriangleup {DHG}} = \dfrac{1}{4}{S}_{ABCD}$ .

于是 ${S}_{\square {EFGH}} = \dfrac{1}{2}{S}_{ABCD} = \dfrac{1}{2}{EG} \cdot  {HF}\sin \alpha$

$\Rightarrow  {S}_{ABCD} = {EG} \cdot  {HF}\sin \alpha  \leq  {EG} \cdot  {HF}$ ; 设 $M$ 为 ${BD}$ 的中点,则 ${HM} = \dfrac{1}{2}{AB}$ , ${FM} = \dfrac{1}{2}{CD} \Rightarrow  {HF} \leq  \dfrac{1}{2}\left( {{AB} + {CD}}\right)$ ,同理可得 ${EG} \leq  \dfrac{1}{2}\left( {{AD} + {BC}}\right)$ .

综上,所以 ${S}_{ABCD} \leq  {EG} \cdot  {HF} \leq  \dfrac{1}{2}\left( {{AB} + {CD}}\right)  \cdot  \dfrac{1}{2}\left( {{AD} + {BC}}\right)$ .
