## 题目
求证：$\sin {3\theta } = 4\sin \theta \sin \left( {\dfrac{\pi }{3} + \theta }\right) \sin \left( {\dfrac{2\pi }{3} + \theta }\right)$。

## 答案
$\sin {3\theta } = 4\sin \theta \sin \left( {\dfrac{\pi }{3} + \theta }\right) \sin \left( {\dfrac{2\pi }{3} + \theta }\right)$

## 解析
$4\sin \theta \sin \left( {\dfrac{\pi }{3} + \theta }\right) \sin \left( {\dfrac{2\pi }{3} + \theta }\right)$

$= 4\sin \theta \left( {\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\cos \theta  + \dfrac{1}{2}\sin \theta }\right) \left( {\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\cos \theta  - \dfrac{1}{2}\sin \theta }\right)  = \sin \theta \left( {3{\cos }^{2}\theta  - {\sin }^{2}\theta }\right)$

$= \sin \theta \left( {2{\cos }^{2}\theta  + \cos {2\theta }}\right)  = \sin {2\theta }\cos \theta  + \cos {2\theta }\sin \theta  = \sin {3\theta }\text{ . }$
