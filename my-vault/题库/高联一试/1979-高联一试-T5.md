## 题目
解方程组：$\left\{  \begin{array}{ll} x - y + z = 1, & \text{ ① } \\  y - z + u = 2, & \text{ ② } \\  z - u + v = 3, & \text{ ③ } \\  u - v + x = 4, & \text{ ④ } \\  v - x + y = 5. & \text{ ⑤ } \end{array}\right.$

## 备注

### AI备注



## 答案
$\left( {x, y, z, u, v}\right)  = \left( {0,6,7,3, - 1}\right)$

## 解析
将方程组顺次相加，得到 $\left\{  \begin{array}{l} x + u = 3, \\  y + v = 5, \\  z + x = 7, \\  u + y = 9, \\  v + z = 6. \end{array}\right.$

所以原方程组的解为 $\left( {x, y, z, u, v}\right)  = \left( {0,6,7,3, - 1}\right)$。
